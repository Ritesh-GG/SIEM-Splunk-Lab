Technique: T1078 - Valid Accounts
Data Source: Windows Security Event Log
EventCodes: 4624 (Success) correlated with 4625 (Failure)
SPL Logic: Accounts with both failed and successful logins 
           indicating possible successful brute force
Severity: Critical
False Positive Risk: Low
Note: In production, normalise account names across log sources
      as Windows logs 4624 and 4625 under different name formats
MITRE ATT&CK URL: attack.mitre.org/techniques/T1078
